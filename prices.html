Prices

Group Walks

Monthly flat discount rate for one dog (playgroup every weekday), $395.00 (about $18.00 dollars a day). Two dogs in the same home, $650.00 (about $31.00 dollars a day).

Less than five days a week $25.00 per walk minimum of three walks a week for one dog, $40.00 per walk minimum of three walks a week for two dogs in the same home.

Billing & Payment Policies

Payment is due on the 1st of the month. Dogs will have a secure space in a group for the month that has already been paid for. I accept credit cards online, checks, Google Checkout and PayPal.

How it all works

Service Agreement

I require all clients to sign a Service Agreement so that all polices and procedures are understood and agreed to as well as giving me permission to care for your dogand to enter your home.

Key Exchange for Dog Walking

I ask that clients give me a key at the initial interview. I am insured and bonded. I will keep your house key and entry codes in a secure location.

Pet Tag Identification & Dog License

The best thing you can do for your pet is to have identification on them in case they get lost. I require that all dogs have identification with a name and phone number. If you are unable to provide a tag or if you will be unreachable during your trip I can provide one for you with my name and phone number as a contact. A dog license and micro chipping is also recommended.

Dog Policies

  • Sociable and friendly dogs only for group walking.
  • Dogs must have good voice recall and be able to be allowed off-leash.
  • Dogs over 9 months must be spayed or neutered.
  • Dogs must have identification tags and/or a dog license.
  • Must be up to date on vaccinations such as Bordetella.
  • Currently on a flea and tick program such as Advantage.
  • I require that all dogs that I walk have basic obedience training to keep walks fun and safe. I will continue to work on your dogs training during walks!
