Prices

Group Walks

Monthly flat rate for one dog (playgroup every weekday), $400.00. Two dogs in the same home, $650.00.

Less than five days a week $25.00 per walk minimum of three walks a week for one dog; $40.00 per walk minimum of three walks a week for two dogs in the same home.

Pet Sitting

$25 per private walk, feeding and/or visit.

Billing & Payment Policies

Payment is due on the 1st of the month. Dogs will have a secure space in a group for the month that has already been paid. I accept cash, checks or credit cards online: Google Checkout and PayPal.

How it all works

Service Agreement

I require all clients to sign a Service Agreement so that all polices and procedures are understood and approved; as well as giving me permission to care for your dog and to enter your home.

Key Exchange for Dog Walking

I ask that clients give me a key at the initial interview. I will keep your house key and entry codes in a secure location.

Pet Tag Identification & Dog License

The best thing you can do for your pet is to have identification on them in case they get lost. I require that all dogs have identification with a name and phone number. A dog license and micro chipping is also recommended.

Dog Policies

  • Sociable and friendly dogs only for group walking.
  • Dogs must have good voice recall and be able to be allowed off-leash.
  • Dogs over 9 months must be spayed or neutered.
  • Dogs must have identification tags and/or a dog license.
  • Must be up to date on vaccinations such as Bordetella.
  • Currently on a flea and tick program such as Advantage.
  • To keep walks fun and safe, I require that all dogs I walk have basic obedience training. I will continue to work on your dogs training during walks.
