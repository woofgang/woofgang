Woofgang is a professional dog walking service.

Woofgang provides daily one-hour off-leash exercise and strives to be your trusted dog care provider.

Woofgang philosophy is quality over quantity, which is why Woofgang prides itself in walking small groups of well-behaved dogs for one hour. Woofgang will provide your dog a responsible, loving, safe, social and fun experience. Woofgang is an insured and licensed business.

About me

I’m Patricia, principle and dog walker for Woofgang. Health is very important to me and it ought to be for your dog. I’ll make sure your dog makes nice friends, gets a great workout and returns home happy and tired. I'm Red Cross trained in Adult/Child CPR and Pet Safety, member of the Crissy Field Dog Group and assist to Angela Gardner, All About Paws.biz. References available upon request.
